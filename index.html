MindTrends

Welcome to MindTrends, an interactive platform designed for Mental Health Researchers exploring conditions across Germany.

  • Switch between 2020 and 2023 to view data specific to each period.
  • Use checkboxes to selectively view or hide data for specific diseases.
  • Hover over any bar in the visualization for a detailed comparison of annual changes in prevalence.
  • Watch as bar colors intensify with increasing prevalence.
